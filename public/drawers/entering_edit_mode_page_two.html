The main area for The Borderland 2025 is within the yellow border. Try to stay within that.

The system will warn you if you are breaking any rules, and will tell you all kinds of things along the way. Try to fix as many issues as possible.
Use the different information layers to find a suitable spot for your camp - for example check the sound guide layer in the bottom right corner.
Do not block the red colored fire roads.
