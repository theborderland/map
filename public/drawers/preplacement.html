Why does the map already look so… alive?

For the past six weeks 100+ Borderlings ran an open experiment called Pre-Placement.
We sketched the first version of:
✅ Fire roads & new blue walking paths (for easier exploring)
✅ Plazas & infrastructure (power nodes, toilets, sanctuary, etc.)
✅ Early “vibe maps” for many neighborhoods—sound zones, chill zones, art clusters

No territory is “reserved.” Everything on the current map is an invitation to collaborate, not a land-grab.
