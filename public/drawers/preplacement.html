Why does the map already look so… alive?

For the past five weeks 150+ Borderlings ran an open experiment called Pre-Placement.
We sketched the first version of:
✅ Fire roads, blue walking paths and plazas
✅ Infrastructure (power nodes, toilets, sanctuary, etc.)
✅ Early “vibe maps” for neighborhoods—sound zones, chill zones, art clusters

No spot is “reserved.” or "claimed". Everything on the current map is an invitation to collaborate, not a land-grab.
