Why is there different colors for camps?

Area type colors show the main function of a location. The default categories are meant to be easy to read at a glance.

  • 💜 Public offering
  • 🩷 Sound stage / sound camp
  • 🩵 Camp communal space / normal camp
  • 🤍 Art piece
  • 🩶 Other
