Your Placement Tools

  1. Click the "Edit" button in the lower left
  2. Start drawing a shape and close it by clicking on the first point
  3. A box will pop up asking for your camp's information. Fill in all fields, including power needs, sound levels, and your contact information (so people can collaborate with you!)
  4. Pay attention to how much space it says your camp needs depending on how many people are in tents or vehicles. Other stuff in m² means anything else, like kitchen tents, domes, artwork, chill spaces etc.
  5. Try using the measurement tool to measure the footprint of your camp or measure distances to toilets or other landmarks.
  6. IMPORTANT! Click the Save-button.
  7. Once placed, you can click on your camp to get an "Edit shape" button to adjust your footprint, move your camp, or edit your camp's info.
  8. Done!

Using Info Layers

Make sure to check out the slope map, sound map and height map and a bunch of other layers in the bottom right menu. The POI layer contains some good-to-know-information!

Using the measure tool

  • Click to add a point

  • To finish drawing a line just doubleclick, or singleclick onto the last point, or press “ESC”-key.
  • Moving of line’s points afterwards is possible by clicking and draging them.
  • To continue a line after it has been finished, hold the Ctrl-Key while clicking onto the first or last point of a line.
  • To add points, hold the Ctrl-Key while clicking onto an arrow.
  • To delete points, hold the Shift-Key while clicking onto a point.
