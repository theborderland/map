Welcome to the interactive placement map!

Placement is a Collaborative Process

No matter how tempting it is to rush in and stake out your territory on the map,
Placement at The Borderland is a collaborative process, not a first-come, first-served land grab.

If this is your first time here, read the instructions by clicking the ℹ️ icon in the top left corner to get to know the process!

Otherwise, have fun, respect other peoples work and remember to refresh the page to see changes!
